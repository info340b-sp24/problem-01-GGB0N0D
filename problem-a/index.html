Spike Spiegel

--from anime Cowboy Bebop

Cowboy Bebop is one of my favorite Japanese anime, and I really admire its protagonist--Spike Spiegel. He is a bounty hunter on a spaceship called Bebop and travels through space with his crew.

    Reasons why I admire Spike
  • He is mysterious. The beginning of the Cowboy Bebop didn't introduce his background story. It is being revealed through multiple episodes, and that makes me interested in the story behind him.
  • He is both physically and mentally strong. He knows how to shoot and fight, that makes most situations safe when I saw him appear. And also he is always calm and chill, his steady emotion makes him reliable.
  • He values his friendship. Although he sometimes will have arguments with his crew, he never really gave up on them. Oppositely, he remembers and cares every friends he met in his heart.
